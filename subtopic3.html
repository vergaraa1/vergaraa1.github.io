Defence and Military Analysis
Site Author
 International Institute for Strategic Studies


Abstract
 The site contains technological advancements made in the U.S. defense industry and globally. It also demonstrates quick numbers on how the United States' defense budget compares to other nations. With a defense budget totalling 968 billion in 2024, it is larger than the next dozen countries combined, as depicted on the graph. This raises the question of where the country stands in priority. Activists have called for the resolution of social problems such as food insecurity, homelessness, or, in this scenario, investing in education to better benefit the country’s technological advancements, steering away from advancements in an arguably destructive field.

 Technology in the Public Sector and the Future of Public Work
Site Author
 Sara Hinkley
Abstract
 Hinkley’s publication discusses how technology is implemented in the public sector, dividing it into five categories: manual task automation, automated decision-making systems, integrated data systems, process automation, and electronic monitoring. However, she also discusses the disadvantages of rapidly implementing these advancements into public policy. With limited resources and staff on hand in the public sector and an increasing U.S. population, implementing advanced technology cannot be done immediately. This can often lead to agencies being left in a worse state and increasing worker burnout. A balance between humans and AI is necessary in the field of government for decision-making, not heavy dependence on one or the other.
